If you have an Orkut account, you can collect, manage and share web widgets with your friends on Orkut using our Orkut application, Hypercube on Orkut.


After you install and run our Orkut application from inside this window, we'll automatically establish a link between Hypercube on Orkut and Hypercube right here on your desktop.


From then on you'll be able to drag-and-drop web widgets in and out of Orkut at any time, as easily as you do with other widget collections.
