Questions

1. Why do some widgets appear in the Widget Gallery with a default Gear icon while some have custom icons?

2. Why does the Hypercube seemingly close by itself sometimes?

3. How do I shut off the sound effects in the Hypercube?

4. When I click "Sign In" in the Google Talk widget, nothing happens – how do I log in?

Answers

1. Why do some widgets appear in the Widget Gallery with a default Gear icon while some have custom icons?

If Amnesty Hypercube cannot download an icon from a widget provider, the icon is generated automatically when the widget is opened for the first time. However, this method only works with some widgets. Widgets that are Flash-based will appear with the default Gear icon. In a future version of Amnesty Hypercube you'll be able to customize your own widget icons.

2. Why does the Hypercube seemingly close by itself sometimes?

If you click on an external web page link inside a widget, the link will be opened in your default web browser. Because your web browser cannot be displayed while the Hypercube is opened, the Hypercube closes when an external link is clicked. We are currently working on a way to minimize this context shift in a future version of Amnesty Hypercube.

3. How do I shut off the sound effects in the Hypercube?

The answer to this question can be found in our Tips & Tricks page.

3. When I click "Sign In" in the Google Talk widget, nothing happens – how do I log in?

The easiest way is to click Create an Account Now, and then click the link that reads sign in here on the page that opens in the the minibrowser. When the Sign In page loads in the minibrowser, enter your information and make sure Remember me on this computer is checked. Once you log in, the minibrowser will close and the widget should automatically log in. In the future you'll be able to log in by clicking "Sign In" in the widget. An alternative solution is to launch Safari and log into your Google account with Remember me on this computer checked.
